Android App for Movus Logistics

This is an Android app developed for tracking company cars. The app allows users to perform actions such as renting a car, returning a car, and viewing the list of available cars. Features: Rent a Car: Allows users to rent a company car by selecting from a list of available cars, providing the destination and purpose of the trip. Real-time Car Availability: Fetches the list of available cars from a remote server to ensure up-to-date information. Integration with Backend: Communicates with a backend server via HTTP requests to fetch car data, insert rental information, and delete car records. User-friendly Interface: Utilizes spinners, text inputs, and dialog boxes to guide users and provide feedback on their actions. See more here: https://github.com/YasinOkat/MovusAndroid
